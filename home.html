Walking home late? Visiting an unfamiliar city? Coffee run at midnight during CalHacks? With the power of the Mapbox API, we have built a map that routes the safest path from where you are to where you want to be! Our backend algorithm combines public crime datasets that help map out the safest possible route.

In the future, we see our app being heavily used by university students, tourists, and in everyday navigation! With more time, we would have liked to have our algorithm rank hazardness by time of day and weight of the crime.
